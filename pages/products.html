Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

Are your products compatible with all elevator brands?

Yes, our products are designed to be compatible with all major elevator manufacturers including Otis, Schindler, KONE, Mitsubishi, and others. We provide detailed compatibility guides and customization options if needed.

Do you provide installation services?

We provide comprehensive installation support including detailed manuals, video guides, and on-site technical assistance. For complex installations, we can recommend certified installation partners across India.

What warranty do you offer on products?

Our products come with a standard 1-year warranty. Extended warranty options (2-3 years) are available for enterprise customers. All warranties include technical support and replacement of defective components.

Can I get customized products for specific requirements?

Absolutely. We specialize in customized solutions for unique elevator projects. Our engineering team can modify existing products or develop new components based on your specific technical requirements and project specifications.
